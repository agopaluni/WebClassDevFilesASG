About Us

Atharv Gopaluni, Sidanth Menon, Aditya Jain, and Rishi Attarde are childhood friends and at their heart, just love talking about random stuff. In this podcast you can witness them delve into their most random thoughts, occasionally providing some weirdly good insight. This is just the video episodes, but there's a ton of audio ones too from before the video days.

You can find them on YouTube, Instagram (for clips and news stories), Spotify, and, for all the audio episodes, their Anchor (now Spotify for Podcasters) page.
