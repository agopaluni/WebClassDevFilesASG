ILL

Directed by: Atharv Gopaluni
Year: 2024
Starring: Atharv Gopaluni
Editors: Atharv Gopaluni

ILL is a story of isolation. A man is trapped inside his home after falling ILL, and soon loses all ability to communicate. Watch and follow his descent into madness over the course of the film.

Viewer Discretion Advised: Language and Themes of Depression, Isolation

A disclaimer: This film is not easy to watch. It is uncomfortable. It is like a podcast in a dystopian world. It is long, it is hard to watch, but it is what it is.
