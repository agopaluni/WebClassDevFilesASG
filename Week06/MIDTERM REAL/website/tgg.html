About Us

Atharv Gopaluni and Aditya Jain are car enthusiasts at heart. Both of them got their obsessions with cars from the (masterpiece of a) movie, Cars (2006). Ever since then, they've been hooked. Since 2019, Aditya has been following Formula 1, and in 2020, he brought Atharv on board. Since then, they have been closely following the sport. For the 2024 season, they have decided to put together a podcast to share their passion for this sport, including race news, race reviews, and paddock news in general.

You can find them on YouTube, Instagram (for clips and news stories), Spotify, and TikTok (for clips).
